Help

Learning Management System Project

In the "Add task" tab, fill in the specified fields. Then click the "Create task" button to create the task. Don't forget to assign your task to the right user.

After that your task will now appear in the tab "Backlog". There you can click on a task to edit it or to move it to the board.

Once the task is moved to the board, you can now see the task in the tab "Board", where it is now in the column "To Do".

You can now drag the task to one of the other columns ("In progress", "Testing" or "Done") and drop it there to show the progress you've made so far with the specific task.
